Fees

Transfer Fees

The transfer fee when using Brastel Remit will be charged according to the sending amount.

Transfer Amount Transfer Fee
Up to ¥30,000 ¥880
From ¥30,001 to ¥100,000 ¥1,480
From ¥100,001 to ¥500,000 ¥1,680

Other fees

• Refund Fee

A refund fee in the amount of ¥500 + bank transfer fee will apply in case of remittance refund.

• Bank Transfer Fee

Bank transfer fee will apply when refunding a deposit back to the sender's bank account, or when transferring
the sending amount to the Brastel Remit account for the remittance.

• Records Inquiry / Amendment Fee

An amount of ¥500 will apply to process record inquiries and amendments.

• Withdrawal Fees and Taxes

Withdrawal fees and/or local taxes may apply at the remittance destination.

Remittance Restrictions

Max. remittance amount per transaction ¥500,000
Max. remittance amount a day ¥3,000,000
Max. remittance amount a month ¥3,000,000
Max. transactions a day 30 transactions
Max. transactions a month 30 transactions
