Application form Step 2/5

To apply for the Brastel Remit service you must provide ID verification documents.
Please choose one of the methods and follow the instructions:

Within a week, you will receive your username and temporary sign in password (by restricted delivery service) to activate your account and start using the Brastel Remit service.
You will be requested by the deliverer to present one of the following documents:

  • Valid Passport
  • Alien Registration Card
  • Driver's License
  • Insurance Card
  • Pension Book

After submitting the online form, you must send us your ID verification documents to finalize your subscription.
You may submit one of the following documents:

  • Copy of the Alien Registration Card (both sides) or Residence Card (both sides)
  • Copy of the driver's license (both sides)
  • Copy of the Resident Registration Card (with photo)
  • Residence Certificate (original, issued within the last 3 months)

You may also submit a copy of your passport (within validation, pages that contain your name, photo, passport number, expiration date and signature), insurance card or pension book PLUS utility payment receipt (recent three months).

Scan or take a picture of one of the following documents and upload it to our server.
The file must be saved as JPEG, PNG, GIF or PDF format (file size must be less than 2MB).

  • Copy of the Alien Registration Card (both sides) or Residence Card (both sides)
  • Copy of the driver's license (both sides)
  • Copy of the Resident Registration Card (with photo)
  • Residence Certificate (original, issued within the last 3 months)

You may also submit a copy of your passport (within validation, pages that contain your name, photo, passport number, expiration date and signature), insurance card or pension book PLUS utility payment receipt (recent three months).
